Our Story

A Taste of Refinement
in the Nilgiris

Tucked away in the sleepy hill town of Coonoor, Elegancia is more than a restaurant — it is a quiet celebration of the art of dining. Old-school British vibes meet intercontinental craftsmanship, all framed by panoramic views of the Nilgiri mountains.

From hand-rolled pastas to slow-simmered rogan josh, every plate is composed with care by a kitchen that treats each guest as the evening's only one. Candlelight, curated music, warm service, and a window seat that catches the sunset — this is the Elegancia experience.

The Kitchen

Crafted by Hand,
Plated with Soul

Our kitchen is small, deliberate and proud. Local produce, whole spices, long reductions and honest technique — we cook the way we wish to be cooked for. No shortcuts, no compromises, only the unwavering pursuit of flavour.
